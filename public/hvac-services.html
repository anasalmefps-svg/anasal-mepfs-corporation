HVAC / Mechanical Ventilation Services

Our HVAC and Mechanical Ventilation services provide professional installation of air distribution systems used in commercial, industrial, and residential buildings. These systems help maintain proper airflow, temperature control, and indoor air quality.

Through properly designed ventilation systems, air can circulate efficiently inside buildings, ensuring comfort, safety, and energy efficiency.

Scope of Works

  • HVAC ducting installation
  • Air distribution systems
  • Ventilation duct layout
  • Duct insulation installation
  • Air balancing and testing
  • System commissioning

Why It Is Important

A properly installed HVAC ducting system ensures efficient airflow, improved indoor air quality, and comfortable room temperatures. Mechanical ventilation systems also remove heat, smoke, dust, and contaminated air from enclosed spaces.

Ducting Installation

Ducting installation is an essential part of HVAC systems that distributes conditioned air throughout a building. These ducts carry cooled or heated air from the HVAC equipment to different rooms and spaces.

Our ducting systems are designed to provide balanced airflow, reduce energy loss, and ensure long-term durability.

Scope of Works

  • Fabrication of air ducts
  • Installation of supply and return ducts
  • Duct insulation installation
  • Installation of diffusers and grilles
  • Air leakage testing
  • System commissioning

Why It Is Important

Proper ducting installation ensures efficient airflow distribution and improves the overall performance of HVAC systems. It helps maintain comfortable indoor temperatures while reducing energy consumption.
