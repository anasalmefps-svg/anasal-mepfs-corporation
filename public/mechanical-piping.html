Mechanical & Utility Piping Services

Our Mechanical and Utility Piping Services provide professional installation, design, and maintenance of specialized piping systems used in industrial, commercial, and healthcare facilities.

These systems ensure the safe and efficient transport of gases and utilities such as LPG and medical oxygen, which are essential for operational safety and building functionality.

Scope of Works

  • LPG Piping Installation
  • Medical Oxygen Piping Systems
  • Mechanical Utility Piping Layout
  • Pressure Testing
  • Leak Detection
  • System Commissioning

Why It Is Important

Properly installed piping systems ensure safe gas distribution and reliable facility operations. In hospitals, medical oxygen piping is critical for patient care, while LPG piping systems provide efficient fuel supply for commercial kitchens and industrial operations.

LPG Piping Installation

Our LPG Piping Installation service provides professional design, installation, and testing of Liquefied Petroleum Gas piping systems used in commercial kitchens, restaurants, industrial facilities, and residential buildings.

The system ensures safe and efficient delivery of LPG fuel from the storage tank to cooking equipment or gas-powered appliances while complying with safety and engineering standards.

Scope of Works

  • LPG piping layout and design
  • Installation of gas pipelines
  • Pressure testing and leak detection
  • Installation of regulators and valves
  • Gas tank connection and integration
  • System commissioning and safety inspection

Why It Is Important

Proper LPG piping installation ensures safe gas distribution and prevents leaks or hazards. A well-designed system improves operational efficiency while maintaining high safety standards for commercial and industrial facilities.

Medical Oxygen Piping System

Our Medical Oxygen Piping System service provides the design, installation, and maintenance of medical gas pipelines used in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities.

The system distributes oxygen safely from the central supply source to patient rooms, operating rooms, and critical care areas, ensuring a continuous and reliable oxygen supply.

Scope of Works

  • Medical gas pipeline design
  • Oxygen piping installation
  • Outlet and regulator installation
  • Leak testing and pressure testing
  • System commissioning
  • Inspection and preventive maintenance

Why It Is Important

Medical oxygen piping systems are critical for patient care in healthcare facilities. A properly installed system ensures safe, uninterrupted oxygen delivery to medical equipment and patients during treatment and emergencies.
