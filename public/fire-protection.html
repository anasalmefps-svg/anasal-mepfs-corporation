Professional fire protection systems for critical facilities

What is Fire Protection & Life Safety?

Fire Protection & Life Safety is a critical engineering discipline focused on the safeguarding of human life and property from the destructive effects of fire and smoke.

It involves the strategic integration of active systems—such as sprinklers, alarms, and suppression units—and passive features—like fire-resistant walls and escape routes. The primary goal is to detect fires early, control or extinguish them rapidly, and ensure the safe evacuation of occupants, minimizing risk and ensuring business continuity.

Core Objectives

  • Life Safety & Evacuation
  • Property Protection
  • Business Continuity
  • Environmental Protection
  • Early Fire Detection
  • Suppression & Control

Kitchen Fire Suppression System

Our Kitchen Fire Suppression System service provides complete design, supply, installation, testing, and maintenance of automatic fire suppression systems for commercial and industrial kitchens.

The system is engineered to rapidly detect and suppress grease and flame fires originating from cooking equipment, exhaust hoods, and ductwork, helping prevent fire spread and severe property damage.

Scope of Works

  • Site survey and risk assessment
  • System design and layout
  • Installation of nozzles and piping
  • Gas and electrical shut-off integration
  • Functional testing and commissioning
  • Preventive maintenance programs

Why It Is Important

Commercial kitchens are exposed to high fire risk due to grease build-up and open flames. A properly designed suppression system automatically discharges wet chemical agents that cool hot surfaces, suppress flames, and prevent re-ignition.

Fire Sprinkler System

Our Fire Sprinkler System service covers the engineering, supply, installation, testing, and maintenance of automatic water-based fire protection systems for commercial, residential, and industrial buildings.

The system is designed to control or suppress fire at its early stage by discharging water directly at the seat of the fire, significantly reducing fire spread and structural damage.

Scope of Works

  • Hazard classification and system design
  • Hydraulic calculations and layout preparation
  • Installation of piping, valves, and sprinkler heads
  • Fire pump, tank, and controller integration
  • Pressure testing and system commissioning
  • Routine inspection and maintenance services

Why It Is Important

Fire sprinkler systems are one of the most reliable life-safety systems available. They help control fire development, protect occupants during evacuation, and minimize damage to property and critical equipment.

Fire Alarm System

Our Fire Alarm System service provides complete design, installation, testing, and maintenance of conventional and addressable fire detection and alarm systems for commercial and industrial facilities.

The system provides early detection of smoke, heat, or fire and delivers audible and visual warnings to occupants, enabling fast evacuation and emergency response.

Scope of Works

  • System planning and device layout
  • Installation of fire alarm control panels
  • Smoke and heat detector installation
  • Manual call points, sounders, and strobes
  • System programming and commissioning
  • Periodic testing and preventive maintenance

Why It Is Important

A properly installed fire alarm system provides early warning during emergencies, allowing occupants to evacuate safely and helping fire responders act quickly, greatly reducing the risk of injury, loss of life, and extensive property damage.
